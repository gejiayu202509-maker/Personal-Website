成长感悟 Reflections

初中三年的核心收获不是某个奖项或某次荣誉，而是在服务他人的过程中理解了"责任"的实际含义。团支部书记和体育委员不是虚衔——需要切实解决问题、分配任务、对结果负责。这种训练比任何课堂知识都更直接地影响了后来的做事方式。

The core takeaway from junior high wasn't a particular award or honor — it was understanding what "responsibility" actually means through serving others. Class secretary and PE rep weren't empty titles — they required solving real problems, delegating tasks, and being accountable for outcomes. That training shaped my approach to everything that followed more directly than any classroom lesson.
