成人礼 · Coming-of-Age
成人礼 · Coming-of-Age

我以优异成绩从海南中学初中部升入本校高中部，进入了理科实验班——1班。那里是全年级最顶尖的集合，每个人都很强。而我，泯于众人。

理科成绩一塌糊涂，排名一度滑落至倒数。我开始迷茫，开始怀疑自己——是不是我本来就不行？是不是这个体系已经给出了答案？

I entered the high school division of Hainan Middle School with strong grades, placed into Class 1 — the elite science class. It was the most competitive group in the grade. And there, I disappeared into the crowd. My science grades crashed. My ranking slid to the bottom. I started doubting myself — was I just not good enough? Had the system already given me my answer?

高一升高二的暑假，我参加了在香港的职场体验项目。那是我第一次置身于完全不同的世界——国际化的氛围、亚洲金融中心的脉动、多元文化的碰撞。我突然意识到，人生的评价标准不止一种。

我开始思考：我适合什么样的体系？我想要什么样的教育？我需要离开这片以成绩定终生的土壤——一个教条的、死板的地方。我需要的不是入场券，而是离场券。我需要自由。

The summer between 10th and 11th grade, I joined a career immersion program in Hong Kong. For the first time, I was in a completely different world — international atmosphere, the pulse of an Asian financial hub, the collision of cultures. It hit me: there's more than one way to measure a life. I began asking: what kind of education suits me? What kind of system do I want? I needed to leave a place where a single exam score defined your entire worth — a rigid, dogmatic soil. What I needed wasn't an entry ticket. It was an exit ticket. I needed freedom.

但我没有一味幻想，也没有摆烂。我很清楚：要离开，就必须先在这套规则里赢。高考成绩就是我的离场券——不是入场券，是离场券。我要用最硬的成绩，换最自由的选择。

But I didn't just daydream or give up. I knew clearly: to leave, I had to first win by the rules of this game. My Gaokao score would be my exit ticket — not an entrance ticket, an exit ticket. I would use the hardest results to buy the freest choice.

Over three years of high school, I learned one thing: no one gets to define me. If anyone does, it's me.

From the bottom of the elite science class to IELTS 7.0, AST 710, and Gaokao top 0.3% province-wide. From a lost student in Class 1 to a speaker on stage sharing experience. From someone who wanted to escape to someone who earned an "exit ticket" through sheer effort and then chose their own path.

双保险不是走捷径，是把两条看似很难相交的路，踏成一条属于自己的坦途。

Dual insurance isn't taking a shortcut. It's turning two paths that seem impossible to intersect into one broad road that belongs to you alone.
